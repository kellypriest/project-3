INTAKE FORM

Adoption Intake Model

The animal rescue community is always trying to find ways to advocate for their animals. Shelters can easily become overwhelmed with the number of animals in their care, if they take on animals that their facility cannot easily place. We wanted to build a tool that could predict the outcome of an animal at a facility. Using dog outcome data from the Department of Animal Services in Louisville, Kentucky, we built a model that would let a rescue know to deny or accept a dog based on their predicted outcome.
